## How It Works

This command invokes the `financial-coach` skill, which:

1. **Fetches context once** (Year Plan, Vision, Golden Rules, Founder Wisdom)
2. **Analyzes your question** OR asks you one sharp financial question
3. **Asks 1-2 follow-ups max** (if critical financial data needed)
4. **Gives direct coaching** with ONE specific financial action

**Time**: < 2 minutes

**Output**: Console only (no Notion saving). Direct, financial, action-oriented.

## Framework Foundations

### Base/Best/Worst Case Planning (Stage 2 Capital)

Three parallel scenarios that adapt to reality:
- **Base Case**: Built from historical data + realistic assumptions
- **Best Case**: Upside scenario (partnerships scale, pricing works)
- **Worst Case**: Downside planning (churn rises, delays happen)

Track monthly to know which scenario you're in.

### Pieter Levels' Bootstrapping Framework

6-Stage Process:
1. **IDEA**: Solve your own problems
2. **BUILD**: Ship in days/weeks, not months
3. **LAUNCH**: Fast and minimal
4. **GROW**: Organically through quality
5. **MONETIZE**: Charge ASAP
6. **AUTOMATE**: Build automation from day one

Reality: Most indie makers launch 10-30 products before breakthrough.

### 30/60/90 Day Rolling Framework

Ditch the dusty annual plan:
- **Next 30 Days**: 3-5 big bets, clear revenue targets, specific sales activities
- **Next 60 Days**: Pipeline building, deals to nurture
- **Next 90 Days**: Strategic bets, hiring, partnerships

Block sales time FIRST, plan everything else around it.

## Differences from Other Coaches

### Financial Coach vs Executive Coach

**Financial Coach**: Numbers, burn rate, revenue, sales activities, survival
- "What's your burn rate vs runway?"
- "How many sales calls this week?"
- "Base/Best/Worst case scenarios"

**Executive Coach**: Strategic alignment, OKRs, focus, decision-making
- "Does this move your Q1 OKR?"
- "What's the real constraint?"
- "Kill this or keep it?"

### Financial Coach vs Levels Coach

**Financial Coach**: Bootstrap-friendly financial planning + sales execution
- Burn rate, runway, revenue targets
- 30/60/90 rolling planning
- Sales-first activities

**Levels Coach**: Pure shipping, anti-overthinking, build/launch fast
- "Ship this week"
- "No VC, bootstrap"
- "Automate > hire"

**Overlap**: Both emphasize bootstrapping, both push for action over planning
